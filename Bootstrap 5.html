Sunf Flowers

Sunflowers belong to the Asteraceae family and are native to North America. They are annual plants, which means they complete their lifecycle in a single growing season. The flower head of a sunflower is actually composed of numerous small flowers called florets. The central part of the flower head is made up of disk florets, and the outer "petals" are actually individual ray florets.

Diversity

Birds are found all over the world, from the Arctic to the Antarctic, in forests, deserts, oceans, and even cities. There are around 10,000 species of birds, each adapted to their specific habitats and lifestyles.Birds inhabit nearly every type of ecosystem on Earth, from polar regions to tropical rainforests, deserts to grasslands, oceans to urban environments. Each habitat presents its own challenges and opportunities for survival. Birds have evolved a multitude of adaptations to suit their specific habitats and lifestyles. These adaptations include beak shape, wing size, plumage coloration, and behaviors.
